[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
When the neon flickers out across the county line
I got nothing on my mind but making you mine
Just a dusty road, a porch light, and a midnight breeze
Come a little closer, baby, let the world freeze

[Verse 1]
The crickets sing a lullaby down in the tall grass
Watching the shadows in your eyes slowly pass
I got a blanket in the back of my old Chevrolet
Let's drive out where the city lights fade away

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath you take is pulling me deep
Awaking a fire that was falling asleep
Your hand in my hand, I don't need a word
It's the sweetest silence I have ever heard

[Chorus]
Oh, this is our trailer park serenade under the moon
Dancing in the dust to a late-night tune
I want to lose myself in the warmth of your skin
Tracing every line where your heart begins

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, where your heart begins, baby
[Echo] (Where your heart begins)

[Verse 2]
I pour a little whiskey in a single glass
We sip it slow and let the hours pass
The way you bite your lip when you look at me
Is writing a song in my memory

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, where your heart begins, baby
[Echo] (Where your heart begins)

[Bridge]
Through the unseen light and the silent fire
You are the only truth I desire
Say my name softly, make it all feel right
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...) Saeed tonight

[Spoken]

Just you, me, and this midnight sky
